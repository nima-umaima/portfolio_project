Our Story

The Blessed Hope Centre Buruburu, which is a branch of the Redeemed Gospel Church Inc. was founded by Rev. Felix Kavoi together with his wife Pastor Anne Kavoi on the 10th of August 1997. From a very humble background, the church has risen to approximately over a thousand members currently.
